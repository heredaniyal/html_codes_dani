CHAI MAKING RECIPE

In this recipe i will show you how to make great chai (we don't call it tea here in pakistan).

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea Leaves/Bags
  • Milk
  • Sugar (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the Water in a Kettle
  2. Pour Tea Leaves into the Kettle or if you have tea bags then pour water into a mug then add tea bags and stir them.
  3. Add half Tea Water and half milk (depends if you want strong or light tea)
  4. Add 2 tea spoon of sugar and mix well
  5. As we say in Pakistan, ye lein apki garma garm chai
